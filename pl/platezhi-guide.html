Payments: Deposit and Withdrawal

Uzcard, Humo, Payme, Click, cards and crypto

This section answers the most frequent questions: how to deposit and withdraw, limits and fees, why payments are sometimes delayed. We cover Uzcard, Humo, Payme, Click, Visa/Mastercard and crypto (BTC/USDT). Examples use €, but the steps are the same for any currency.

Uzcard: How to Deposit → How to Withdraw

Deposit

  1. Open the cashier and select Uzcard.
  2. Enter the amount and confirm the payment.
  3. Wait for crediting (usually instant).

Withdrawal

  1. Go to the Withdrawal section.
  2. Select Uzcard and amount.
  3. Confirm the request and wait 1–24 hours.
Tip: pass KYC before your first withdrawal to speed things up.

Humo: How to Deposit → How to Withdraw

The process is similar to Uzcard. The difference is possible bank restrictions on online transactions.

  1. Select Humo in the cashier.
  2. Enter the amount and confirm via SMS code.
  3. For withdrawal use the same method.

Payme and Click

E-wallets are convenient for quick operations. Flow: select service, confirm in app, wait for crediting.

  1. Select Payme or Click.
  2. Enter the amount and confirm in the app.
  3. Withdrawal — same flow, same wallet.
Important: services may have limits on amount and frequency.

Visa/Mastercard

Cards are universal, but withdrawal can be slower (up to 5 days). Some banks require confirmation for international transactions.

  1. Select card in the cashier.
  2. Enter card details and confirm payment.
  3. For withdrawal use the same card.
Tip: if payment is declined, call your bank and ask to unblock gambling transactions.

Bitcoin / USDT

Crypto allows fast withdrawal but requires care with address and network. For USDT different networks (TRC20, ERC20) are often available, and the choice affects the fee.

  1. Select crypto method and network.
  2. Copy the address and send funds.
  3. For withdrawal enter your wallet and wait for network confirmations.
Important: wrong address or network can lead to loss of funds.

Limits and Fees

Typical limits: up to 500 € per day and 2,500 € per month. Fees are often zero on the casino side, but your bank or crypto network may charge. So check your bank's terms before a large deposit.

What to Do If Payment Fails

  • Check your card or wallet limits.
  • Verify the data is correct.
  • Try again in 10–15 minutes.
  • Contact support with the amount and time of the transaction.

Most often the issue is resolved by changing method or confirming the transaction with your bank.
