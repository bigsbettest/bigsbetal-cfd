BigsBet Registration: Complete Guide from A to Z

Registration: Step-by-step Guide

Account, currency, KYC and 2FA

This guide explains registration in detail: four ways to create an account, currency selection, KYC verification, enabling 2FA and password recovery. Written for beginners from CIS, including users from Tashkent, Samarkand, Bishkek and Baku. Example currency — €, but you can choose another at registration.

4 Registration Methods

BigsBet typically offers several sign-up options: via email, phone, social networks and a simplified “one-click” option. Choose the one that best fits future account recovery.

Method 1: Email

  1. Open the site and click “Register”.
  2. Enter your email and create a strong password.
  3. Confirm your email via the link.
  4. Complete your profile: name, date of birth, address.
Important: use a real email. It receives bonus confirmations and withdrawal notifications.

Method 2: Phone

  1. Select phone registration.
  2. Enter your number and wait for the SMS code.
  3. Create a password and confirm sign-in.
Tip: keep your phone number in your profile — it speeds up account recovery.

Method 3: Social Networks

Registration via social networks is sometimes available. It is faster but ties your account to an external service. Good for users who change devices often and want 1–2 click sign-in.

Method 4: One Click

Simplified registration usually requires minimal data. The downside: you will still need to complete your profile for withdrawals later.

Account Currency Selection

Currency choice affects fees, limits and payment convenience. If you use Uzcard, Payme or Click, choose local currency or € to avoid conversion. If planning crypto — consider USDT or BTC, but be aware of network fees.

Important: currency is set at registration. To change it you usually need to create a new account.

KYC Verification

KYC is standard identity verification. It is required for security and withdrawals. Usually they request an ID document, proof of address and sometimes a selfie.

  1. Upload a photo of your passport or ID card.
  2. Add proof of address (utility bill, bank statement).
  3. Wait for verification (usually 24–48 hours).
Tip: scan documents in good quality, without glare or cropped edges.

2FA: Why and How to Enable

Two-factor authentication protects your account from hacking. Enabling takes 2–3 minutes and significantly improves security.

  1. Go to “Security settings”.
  2. Select authenticator app.
  3. Scan the QR code and save the backup key.
Important: without the backup code, 2FA recovery can take time.

Password Recovery

If you forgot your password, use the recovery form. For email registration — email link; for phone — SMS. Make sure contact details are up to date.

  1. Click “Forgot password?”
  2. Enter your email or phone number.
  3. Confirm the code and set a new password.

Common Registration Mistakes

  • Wrong email or phone number entered.
  • Password too weak and fails validation.
  • Profile not completed before first withdrawal.
  • Attempting to change currency after registration.

To avoid issues, complete your profile immediately, use real data and store 2FA backup codes safely.
