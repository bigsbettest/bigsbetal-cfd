Welcome Bonus: Step-by-step Activation

  1. Register and confirm contact details.
  2. Make your first deposit of 5 € or more.
  3. Ensure the bonus is credited to your balance.
  4. Choose slots with full wagering contribution.
Important: if you withdraw before completing wagering, the bonus is forfeited.

Free Spins: Terms and Slots

Free spins are often tied to specific slots. Usually popular games with clear mechanics. Check the wagering weight of free spins and any max win cap.

Cashback: How It Works

Cashback is calculated on net loss for the week. For example, if you lost 200 € and cashback is 10%, you get 20 € back. If you are in profit, no cashback.

Important: cashback often comes without wagering, but check terms in advance.

VIP Programme

VIP levels give higher limits, personal bonuses and priority support. Status depends on bet turnover, so it suits active players. For beginners VIP is optional, but it helps to know its benefits.

Should You Take a Bonus?

A bonus pays off if you are ready to play long term and stay disciplined. If you want to withdraw quickly, play without a bonus. Consider your play style and the time you can spend on wagering.
